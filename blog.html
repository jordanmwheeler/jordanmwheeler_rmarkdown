Pre-TransNebraska Run (1 Day Out)

Date:

August 10, 2018 | 06:38 PM MT

Publisher:

Jordan Wheeler

Post:

This we be a blog containing my daily experiences from my TransNebraska Run. You can also follow my run on my Strava account.

I will keep the blog posts short and simple since I will want to relax and get to bed early each night. As for some logistics about the run you can check out the TransNebraska Route page. I will mainly be running on Highway 26, 30, 34 the entire way, so if I pass by a town you are in, feel free to come join for however long you would like!

As for the structure of each day, I will hopefully stay pretty consistent. I plan to wake up each morning around 3-3:30 AM, and get started on the run by 3:30-4 AM. I plan to run for about 40 miles until around 10:30-11 AM. At this point, I will take a quick lunch break, charge my GPS watch and get going for the second part of the day by 11-11:30 AM. I then plan to run 20-25 miles until about 2:30-3 PM and then finish the day with a 5-10 mile walk and be finished by 5 PM. This will allow me get to bed around 7 PM each night.

I will also be doing my best to post photos each day, to check those out go to the TransNebraska Phots page.
